What is EnrollEngineer?

EnrollEngineer is a startup founded by students and working engineers dedicated to guiding students in their career choices, particularly in selecting appropriate engineering streams. They primarily focus on students from rural areas who often miss out on good opportunities due to a lack of information and cannot afford the high Counselling fees in cities. EnrollEngineer bridges this gap by offering affordable Counselling services, ensuring that these students receive the guidance they need to make informed career decisions. By joining EnrollEngineer, students gain access to personalized advice and structured career planning, empowering them to achieve their full potential. EnrollEngineer's mission is to make quality career Counselling accessible and affordable for every aspiring engineer.

Our Vision

The vision of EnrollEngineer is to empower students to reach their full potential by providing comprehensive and accessible career Counselling. They aspire to be the leading career consultation service, particularly for students from rural areas who often lack access to quality information and guidance. EnrollEngineer is committed to bridging the gap between aspirations and opportunities by offering affordable and personalized Counselling services. Their goal is to create a future where every student, regardless of their background or financial situation, can make informed career choices and succeed in their chosen engineering streams. By fostering a supportive and informed community, EnrollEngineer strives to ensure that every student has the tools and confidence to achieve their career ambitions.
